Restoring Purchases

If you've previously purchased the full game and need to restore it on a new device, tap the Restore Purchases button on the Donutropolis map screen. Your purchase will be restored at no charge.

How Do Hints Work?

Hints are available for players who get stuck. First, reveal the locations of the machines needed to solve the level. Then, reveal specific machines and their configurations one at a time until you’re ready to continue on your own.

How Do I Earn Coins?

You earn coins each time you successfully finish a level. Coins can also be purchased in the game if you need more.
