Restoring Purchases

If you've previously purchased the full game and need to restore it on a new device, tap the Restore Purchases button on the Donutropolis map screen. Your purchase will be restored at no charge.

How Do Hints Work?

Hints cost 100 coins and show you the solution locations for all machines in the current level, color-coded by machine type. You can then tap any hint tile to reveal the specific machine and its configuration for an additional 20 coins.

How Do I Earn Coins?

You earn coins each time you successfully ship a donut order. Coins can also be purchased in the game if you need more.
