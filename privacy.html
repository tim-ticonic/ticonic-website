Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Ticonic Software LLC ("we", "us") built Rocknor & the Donut Factory as a paid app. This page describes our policies regarding the collection and use of information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We collect anonymous gameplay analytics including level starts, completions, resets, and in-app settings changes. This data is associated with a randomly generated identifier and cannot be used to identify you personally.

We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect this data. Firebase may collect additional anonymous device information such as device type and operating system version. For more information see Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

iCloud

If you have iCloud enabled, your game progress and settings are synced across your devices using your personal iCloud account. This data includes level save files and game settings. It is used solely to provide game functionality and is never accessed by us for any other purpose.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are handled entirely by Apple. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@ticonicsoftware.com.
